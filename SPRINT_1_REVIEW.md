### ✅ SPRINT 1 IS APPROVED. SPRINT 2 IS AUTHORIZED.

### What was delivered in Sprint 1

| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| People module | 7 person types, 20-field Isar model, CRUD, search, sort, filter |
| Forms | Add/Edit with validation, duplicate detection, opening balance |
| Detail page | Profile with info cards + 4 placeholder sections (Ledger, Reminders, Share, Stats) |

| Navigation | People tab in bottom nav, person detail via push |
| Tests | 69 tests — repository, validation, search, sort, filter, CRUD |
| Documentation | README, CHANGELOG, architecture, database, 6 files updated |

### What's ready for Sprint 2

The foundation — Accounts, Categories, Transactions, People, Dashboard — is stable, tested, and documented. The architecture lock report's 6 blockers were resolved during Sprint 0.5 hardening. People module (Sprint 1) is complete with zero errors, 69 tests, and APK verified on emulator.

### Recommended focus for Sprint 2

- **Ledger Engine** — Give/Receive Money, Sale/Purchase, Ledger History with running balance
- **Ledger Entry forms** — Record Give, Receive, Sale, Purchase, Adjustment, Discount
- **Outstanding balance calculation** — Computed from entry sums, displayed on person detail
- **Settlement flow** — Link Receive to Give, partial payments, full settlement
