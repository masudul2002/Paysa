## Versioning Strategy
- Documentation version follows product planning milestones.
- Each document maintains its own changelog.
- Approved decisions are not removed; they are superseded with date and rationale.
- Future sync assumptions remain explicitly labeled until implemented.

## Review Cadence
- P0: every milestone and before implementation begins.
- P1: before release candidate.
- P2: monthly or during planning.

## Approval Roles
- Product Manager approves product scope and roadmap.
- Lead Architect approves architecture and dependency decisions.
- Mobile Lead approves app structure, state, navigation, and performance decisions.
- QA Lead approves testability and release gates.
- Security Lead approves privacy, sensitive data, logging, and dependency risk.
